03 How I Design Systems

My approach to platform engineering

01
Understand Scale Constraints Early
Map expected load, data volume, and latency requirements before writing a single line of code.
02
Design for Horizontal Scaling
Stateless services, connection pooling, read replicas, and cache layers that scale with demand.
03
Keep Services Loosely Coupled
Clear API contracts, event-driven communication, and independent deployability across services.
04
Build Observability Into the System
Structured logging, distributed tracing, health checks, and alerting baked in from day one.
05
Automate Operations
CI/CD pipelines, infrastructure as code, automated testing, and self-healing infrastructure.
06
Optimize Cost Continuously
Regular infrastructure audits, usage-based scaling, caching strategies, and architectural efficiency reviews.
